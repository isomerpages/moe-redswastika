<p><strong>Our Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Through the purposeful use of information and communications technology,
we nurture lifelong learners and innovative leaders grounded in sound values.
Our students develop essential digital literacy and technological skills
to navigate the digital world confidently, while remaining committed to
contributing positively to the community.</p>

<p><strong>e-Pedagogy</strong>
</p>
<p>e-Pedagogy refers to the purposeful use of technology to design active
and engaging learning experiences. &nbsp;Guided by e-Pedagogy and the Educational
Technology (EdTech) Pedagogical Scaffold (PS), our teachers design differentiated
learning experiences that enhance student engagement, support subject mastery
and develop 21st Century Competencies. Through thoughtfully designed learning
experiences, we aim to develop our students as lifelong learners who find
meaning and joy in learning.</p>

<p><strong>Digital Literacy</strong>
</p>
<p>Beyond using technology for learning, it is equally important for students
to develop the knowledge, skills and dispositions to become innovative
leaders and responsible users of digital technologies for information,
communication and problem-solving.</p>
<p>Complementing our total curriculum approach, our in-house Digital Literacy
Programme equips students with essential digital literacy and technological
skills while supporting teachers in designing purposeful and meaningful
learning experiences.</p>

<p><strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP) – Tinkers to Thinkers</strong>
</p>
<p>As the Chinese saying goes, “学无止境” — learning is lifelong. Guided by this
belief, our Applied Learning Programme (ALP), <strong>Tinkers to Thinkers</strong>,
seeks to nurture the joy of learning in our students through hands-on,
experiential and purposeful activities. This programme provides a safe
and stimulating learning environment for students to tinker, explore, experiment
and innovate. We believe that tinkering is an active thinking process.
Through tinkering, students inquire, test ideas, reflect on outcomes, and
refine their thinking.</p>
<p>Our ALP provides students with the opportunities to tinker with block-based
coding. Through such learning experiences, they develop computational thinking
and 21st Century Competencies, while gaining a deeper understanding of
how coding and technology can be applied meaningfully in real‑world contexts.
Students are challenged to design and create solutions to authentic problems,
fostering creativity, resilience, and problem‑solving skills.</p>
