<h4><strong>From the Principal’s Desk</strong></h4>
<p>Welcome to Red Swastika School!</p>
<p>It is my joy and privilege to welcome you to our Red Swastika School family.
Founded by the World Red Swastika Society in 1951 as an extension of its
charitable mandate, our school was established with the objective of transforming
lives through education. This founding mission continues to inspire us
today, shaping our commitment to nurturing learning, growth, and service
to the community.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Circle.jpg">
</div>
<p>As we honour our heritage, we also look ahead to the future our students
will thrive. To prepare our students for a rapidly changing world, our
revised school vision aspires for every child to be part of a <strong><em>Caring Community</em></strong> and
to grow as <strong><em>Lifelong Learners</em></strong>, <strong><em>Glocal Citizens</em></strong>,
and <strong><em>Innovative Leaders</em></strong>. These elements reflect
both our heritage and our forward-looking aspirations. <em>Caring Community</em> and <em>Innovative Leaders</em> echo
our founders' emphasis on compassion and transformative education, whilst <em>Lifelong Learners</em> and <em>Glocal Citizens</em> emphasise
the competencies needed in today's interconnected world—where adaptability,
curiosity, and the ability to navigate global perspectives whilst staying
rooted in Singapore are essential.</p>
<p>Our shared aspirations are grounded in our revised school motto, <strong>“恭宽信敏惠”</strong>,
a timeless teaching inspired by the wisdom of Confucius more than 2,000
years ago. This timeless wisdom underpins our belief that every RSS student
can cultivate <strong>仁</strong> (benevolence/compassion) through the practice
of five enduring values:</p>

<p>These values are deeply embedded in our daily interactions, guiding the

<p>These beliefs anchor us as we navigate an increasingly complex world.
They remind us to embrace inclusive perspectives, celebrate every child’s
individuality, and give our very best in nurturing the diverse learners
entrusted to our care.</p>
<p><strong>卍慈的孩子是特选的孩子。</strong>As a Special Assistance Plan school, we are
blessed with the responsibility of nurturing students who are confidently
bilingual and who appreciate the richness of the Chinese culture. Just
as importantly, we want our students to grow into gracious individuals
who value diversity and contribute meaningfully to a more caring and inclusive
society.</p>
<p>In raising our <strong>卍慈的孩子</strong>, we are never alone. We are deeply
grateful for the unwavering support of our parents, alumni, partners, and
community. Your trust strengthens our mission, and your partnership empowers
us to continue shaping brighter futures for every child who walks through
our doors.</p>
<p>Here at Red Swastika School, we cherish our heritage and the stories that
have shaped who we are today. As you explore our website, I hope you feel
the warmth of our community and the spirit that makes RSS a special place
for children to learn, grow, and thrive.</p>
<p>Together, let us continue writing new and meaningful chapters in the Red
Swastika School story.</p>
