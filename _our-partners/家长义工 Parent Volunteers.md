<h5><strong>Registration Period: Opens 5 Dec 2025 till 5 Jan 2026.</strong></h5>
<p><strong><u>PARENT VOLUNTEER SCHEME - FOR CHILDREN BORN IN 2021 FOR SCHEDULED REGISTRATION EXERCISE IN 2027 (ADMISSION IN 2028)</u></strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Parents who are accepted as Parent Volunteers will start contributing
the required <strong>80</strong> voluntary hours one and a half year before
the start of the Primary One Registration Exercise. The timing of the voluntary
activities will be before, during school hours or after school hours for
Co-Curriculum Activities (CCAs)/ Learning Journeys.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Red Swastika School is a Special Assistance Plan (SAP) School offering
Higher Chinese Language only for Primary 1 to 4; and Chinese and Higher
Chinese at Primary 5 &amp; 6.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The Parent Volunteer Scheme does not provide a guarantee for a place in
the school but accords only an eligibility to register in Phase 2B when
the requirements of the Parent Volunteer Scheme are fulfilled.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For more information on the Primary One Registration process, please visit
<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/p1-registration" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/p1-registration</a>. To verify the home-school
distance, please refer to the One Map School Query Service <a href="https://www.onemap.gov.sg/#/SchoolQueryInfo" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.onemap.gov.sg/#/SchoolQueryInfo</a> managed
by the Singapore Land Authority (SLA).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents who are interested may apply via this link <a href="https://form.gov.sg/6930f8e682343f3340de6140" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">FormSG</a> 
</p>
</li>
</ol>
