<h1>RSS Three Littles</h1>
<p>Values inculcation @ RSS is done through aligning the school values to
the Three Littles. This approach is drawn from the Xingzhi education philosophy.
The Little Friend, Little Master and Little Teacher personify values that
are relatable and relevant to the students.</p>
